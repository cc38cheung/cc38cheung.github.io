Logo Inspiration

How the logo shows what the company is about

The parrot’s colours are what brings that person in. However, the type style is what makes the logo appear more sophisticated since it looks high-class.

Why the logo is effective for the brand

Because everyone likes sweets, the parrot is more of a sign for kids to see it. However, for adults, the parrot is like a symbol of freedom and so when the logo is shown, it is like a sign of calmness. It is also similar to thinking of the chocolate as a way of taking a break from all the stress around oneself.

How the branding/logo makes us stand out

- The logo is a bird, and that is a rare idea for a company that sells chocolate. Therefore, it is what makes the company succeed because not only will children want to get close, but also attract adults (because older people are interested in new ideas/ new things).
